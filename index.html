STRATEGY FRAMEWORK 1

Grammar: Modals of Advice

GIVING ADVICE (SOFT)

Use SHOULD or OUGHT TO to suggest a good idea. It is not an obligation.

"You should praise your team publicly."


"He shouldn't criticize people aggressively."

DYNAMIC MODELING

Toggle the option to see the grammar structure change.

Advice: Positive

You SHOULD BE honest with your team.

STRATEGY FRAMEWORK 2

Grammar: Modals of Obligation

STRONG OBLIGATION

Use MUST or HAVE TO when something is necessary or required.

"A manager must care personally."


"We have to give honest feedback."
